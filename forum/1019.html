Samurai Archives site search

by kitsuno

I just discovered something interesting to anyone who wants to be
able to do a 'search' within the Samurai Archives site.

If you go to http://www.google.com and input: site:www.samurai-
archives.com into the search box, you can search for any words you
want by putting them before the 'site'.
example: nobunaga site:www.samurai-archives.com brings up all of the
pages on the samurai archives with the term 'nobunaga' on them.
