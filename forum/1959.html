HI!

Shinsengumi were a sort of police setted during bakumatsu jidai to protect Kyoto from the actions of shishis. They were also called WOLVES OF MIBU, because of their belongings to that place.

They were organised in SQUAD (I'm not sure if this is the rel translation from japanese...i'm italian sorry!), and Souji Okita was the captain of the first one.
