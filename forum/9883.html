Re: [samuraihistory] please help

by getsutakezo

Look around a bit and find some designs that you like. I would go to www.samurai-store.com to look at some dimensions and get some ideas. Alot of the strange looking and unique helmets of the sengoku jidai were normal helmets that had a papier machet decoration built on top. You could always have one made out of leather plates connected together and lacquered. There are alot of good books that you could get for inspiration too.
