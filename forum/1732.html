In response to some questions raised by Tom and Carlo I added to
photos to the album. One is of the blade tip and the first several
inches. (Tom, see I am learning. I did not know what a kissaki or a
monouchi is, but I looked them up in a glossary.) Even though it was
taken outside, without flash, there is some glare in this pic. If you
can not see what you are looking for, tell me and I will see if I can
get a clearer photo.

The second pic is of the inside of a flap on the leather scabbard. I
do not know what the characters say. I assume they identify the sword
owner. This photo was taken inside with a flash using a friend's
camera that does a better job on real close shots.

Scott
