2nd Fundraiser Auction for Journal: Sengoku Daimyo Manga

by kitsuno

Although the first auction is still open, I have set up the second
fundraiser auction. This is an auction for 3 Sengoku Daimyo related
manga - Oda Nobunaga, Hojo Soun, and Takeda Shingen.

The Oda Nobunaga manga is the first issue of a series and is also a
first edition, in great condition. Takeda Shingen is issue #5, and is
20 years old, but in good condition, with some signs of wear and age.
Hojo Soun is probably the most impressive (to me at least), it is a
stand-alone issue (and first edition), which covers Hojo Soun's life
from the Onin war onward. It has great artwork, and is from the series
"Sengoku Busho Retsuden".

All three are imported from Japan and in Japanese. Here is the auction
URL for more info and for bidding:

http://cgi.ebay.com/ws/eBayISAPI.dll?ViewItem&item=190125691876

Thanks!
