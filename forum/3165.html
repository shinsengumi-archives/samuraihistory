Excessive quoting brings bad karma

by goldrushg

I would like to politely ask that anyone quoting a message in their
reply please check to make sure that only a brief (and presumably relevant)
portion of text is actually quoted in your reply. I've noticed some folks
are quoting entire messages (some of which have included the entirety of
messages that *they* were replies to). The result is a digest that contains
pages and pages of needless text.

I find some of the messages here quite interesting and I enjoy reading
them...once. But after the fifth, tenth or twentieth time the newness and
interest wanes a bit. ;)

Thanks for your consideration.
