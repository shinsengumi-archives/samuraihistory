Kirk-
The Shinsengumi did exists unfortunately Ruroni Kenshin and
many of the characters in the comixs did not. When the Tokugawa
bakufu began to lose control of the country in the 1850s, Kyoto
became a magnet for Anti-Tokugawa ronin. The Bakufus' hold on the
city became rather tenuous. Thye Shinsegumi was a group of Tokugawa
loyalists banded together to act as counter-terrorists in the capitol.
They were known for their harsh rules and strict
traditionalism. I would recommend the movies "Shinsengumi" with
Mifune, "Shisengumi Shimatsuki" with Ichikawa, "Bakumatsu zankoku
monogatari" and even the recent "Gohatto". There are many more, but
if these stimulate your interest I think you'll find the true story
of the Shinsegumi more interesting than any manga.
