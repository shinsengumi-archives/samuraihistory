Josh, I have sent several of my students through the JET program and everyone of them have had a pleasant experience.
Regarding your dog, it is not the size that matters, it is the fact that you will have to put your pet in quarantine for some time (at your expense)> Imagine the trauma. If there is any way you can leave your dog with your parents or siblings or friend, anything would be better. He won't pick up the language in a year anyway.
