Turnbull Ninja Book

by kurotatsunoshi

Turnbull's book is quite good and likely the best source on the subject
matter in English you are likely to find. While it does have quite a
few pictures of "black PJ clad assassins" and other ahistorical ninja
lore like the straight sword, this is done to illustrate how the ninja
myth wormed its way into popular culture. No book on ninja would be
complete without addressing the wild stories that have sprung up around
them.
