-At long last I have the answer to that old question of mine- Could
Yuya Shiina the bounty hunter gal from Samurai Deepr Kyo really have
possessed such a weapon like that triple rotating barrel flintlock
pistol she used and would she have been in violation of Shogunate
Japanese law at the time?

Thanks to google and wikipedia-- the answers are yes and no.

First- Yes she could have owned such a weapon since multi-barrelled
flintlock pistols were used by many at that time notably pirates
(duck's foot four barrel pistols et al)

Second- No she (and Megeira with his futuristic volley gun) would
have been wirhin Shogunate Law since NobuNaga did not outlaw firearms
and foreigners in Japan until he united the clans after the great war
in 1615. The Battle of Sekigahara was in 1600 and SDK is set to begin
four years after that battle (1604) so at least then Yuya and Megeira
were not breaking any laws of that period in Japanese Shogunate
history.
