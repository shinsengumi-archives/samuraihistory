Hello

I'm Katsuyori Takaeda club in Japan.

Now we are publicly seeking an illustration of Katsuyori that is to be an advertising character(Public Competition for Mascot Design)
We also receive applications from overseas, so if you are interested in this project,
please see our English page that describes application
http://katsuyoritosa.web.fc2.com/characterinternationak.html

(selection method)
The winner will be selected through the public voting at the event for (August 22 )The 400 Year Anniversary After Katuyori's Death (sponsored by the Association of Takeda Katsuyori in Tosa) and will be chosen by the executive board of the association

We are going to commoditize the illustration and sell it in the event on October 10th.(The 400 Year Anniversary event)

We also perform entertainments such as teodori daiko (hand-dancing Japanese drums) that it is said Katsuyori introduced

Many people come to the venue and our Web site to see the publicly sought illustration, so the author will have opportunity to gain publicity
