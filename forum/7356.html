Leeds Royal Armouries Shogun seminars & activities

by naomasa298

I believe Kitsuno has already posted regarding the Shogun exhibition
currently on at the Leeds Royal Armouries in the UK.

The Armouries is holding a professional seminar on August 17th on
Japanese arms & armour collection and display. From the Armouries
website:
"Aimed at Museum professionals with Japanese arms and armour
collections, the sessions will include guidance on identification,
conservation, and display. Attendees will be able to see Shogun and
receive an exhibition catalogue as part of their ticket price. Must be
pre-booked."

In addition, the Armouries are putting on displays of yabusame every
Wednesday and Thursday as well as demonstrations of calligraphy,
suiboku and ikebana, amongst other things.

A family oriented sleep-over event is scheduled for August 13th.

I'll be visiting the exhibition this week and I'll post with details.
