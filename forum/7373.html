Royal Armouries

by Clive Sinclaire

Dear all
You may be interested in the exhibition being held at the Royal Armouries in Leeds (UK) called The Shogun Exhibition which concentrates on the life and achievements of Tokugawa Ieyasu. It was put together in conjunction with the Nikko Toshogu Shrine. Last Sunday there was a one-day seminar comprising of 3 lectures, which I attended. My report on this may be found on the web site www.to-ken.com under Articles.
Clive Sinclaire
