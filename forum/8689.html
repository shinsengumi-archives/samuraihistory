Samurai Fiction 2006 Results

by kitsuno

Here are the results of the judging for the 2006 Samurai Fiction contest:

Fist Place:

Gaki, by Jesse Workman (Genre: Ghost Story)

Runner up, and winner of the Riccardo Tacchini Memorial Award -
Sponsored by Carlo "Tsubame" Tacchini in memory of his brother:

The Faithful, by Nina Boal (Genre: Historical Fiction)

Honorable Mention:

Massugu, by Andy Lowe (Genre: Pulp Fiction)

There was a lot of debate over these three, and there wasn't really a
clear cut winner until the very end.

All of the entries will be temporarily housed here until I can created
a new webpage:

http://www.samurai-archives.com/sfic06/samfic06.html

And for anyone interested in next year's contest, stay tuned for updates!

Thanks!

C. West
