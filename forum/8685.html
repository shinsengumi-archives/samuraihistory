by shikisokuzekukusokuzeshiki8

Assassination of Ii Naosuke is called "Sakuradamongai no hen"
(Sakuradamongai incident)
Sakuradamon(mon means gate) is one of gates of Edo castle.
He was killed at the front of Sakurada gate by Mito and Satsuma clan ronins.
Mito samurai were known as Sonno-Joui(Respect emperor-Anti foreigners)
They were angry at Ii's decision of the Harris Treaty.
