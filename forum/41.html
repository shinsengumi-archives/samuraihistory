Hi Antenor

The Kukishin Ryu likes to use several Bo. Octogonal, I have one made out of
Peach wood. Strange choice I know but it was a gift, the usual Rokushakubo,
and a Bo wihich has nine rings on it. 4 at each end spaced apart evenly, and a
ninth one in the middle. Not too sure why they have the rings, maybe to
reinforce it. They have also in the past used kyushakubo. I tried to twirl one
once. I found it hard being 6ft (180cm)m so how a smaller Japanese managed,
who knows. Not very practical. Mostly the Bo are made from Oak.

Paul Richardson
