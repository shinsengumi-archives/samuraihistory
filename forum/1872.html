As a moderator of this forum, I'd prefer we don't start labeling some
discussions "Not Samurai Related", because then that opens the door
for hundreds of completely off-topic posts. As long as a topic is
even vaguely or marginally related to Japanese history or culture or
Japan or Japanese, it is free to go (and I think that gives a lot of
leeway in discussion, and I'm not against history-related posts that
are more or less off-topic). Allowing a free-reign of completely off-
topic posts opens a can of worms I'd prefer to avoid.
