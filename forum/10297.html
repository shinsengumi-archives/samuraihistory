The Samurai Archives Podcast

by kitsuno

It has taken about a year to finally get everything together (mainly the participants), but the Samurai Archives podcast is now on the air.

The first two episodes are available, and were recorded at the Association of Asian Studies conference in Honolulu, Hawaii, and detailed some of the seminars. The 3rd episode will be coming up next week, and will be a convention wrap up. After the conference series is done, the actual official podcast format will start, with each episode being between 45 minutes and an hour, and will examine different aspects of Japanese history. This will probably start next month, and new episodes should be released every 3-4 weeks, if not more frequently.

Samurai Archives podcast website: http://samuraiarchives.podbean.com/

Subscribe on Itunes: http://bit.ly/i1FIzT

The first two episodes were recorded on location, and edited with free audio software, so the sound quality is to be expected - not great, but hardware and software is being updated, so within the next month or two the sound quality should dramatically increase.

We look forward to feedback, and once the actual podcast kicks off next month, we look forward to participation, questions, feedback, requests for future episodes, etc.

Enjoy!
