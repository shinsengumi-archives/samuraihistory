Check out The Satsuma Rebelion of 1877, the Shimazu
Clan and Saigo Takamori.
If you have a chance see if you can find the book "The
Last Samurai: The Life and Battles of Saigo Takamori"
by Mark Ravina.

As for Katagiri Katsumoto Im not sure were to look. I
do know of some of the samurai known as the "Seven
Spears of Shizugatake" such as Kato Kiyomasa. Maybe if
you start with him the info on Katsumoto will come to
light. Other than that research the Battle of
Shizugatake (1583).

Hope that helps ya.
