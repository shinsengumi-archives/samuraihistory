RE: [samuraihistory] Museums

by Michael Peters

Rod wrote " I was wondering if there are any museums in Japan that
>specialize in western culture dealing with art and so forth."

Yes there are many. They have a museum of western art at Ueno as well as a
regular art museum that has many western items as well. If there is a
western art exhibition on tour, it almost certainly makes a stop in Tokyo.
