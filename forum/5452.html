Re: [samuraihistory] Need information about Old Samurai

by soshuju

Kris or is it Pujo?
As a member of this list you are free to post digital photographs in
the members photo area. From your description it is very hard to say
anything at all about your sword, however if you can share some
pictures with us there are people on the list who are happy to share
their knowledge. You'll need several photos; one of the whole sword in
it's scabbard, one of the naked tang/signature, one of the backside of
the tang, and if possible one of the first five or six inches above the
hamachi and one of the kissaki (preferably a close up showing the
hamon). Kantei from photos is always pretty dicey but as I said we are
happy to share what we can.
Tom

PS the powder is likely uchiko which is used to clean the blade...
