Re: [samuraihistory] Samurai Armour

by Michael Peters

Takara,

Specifics make things much easier. :) That being said, how big are you?
Unless you are fairly small antiques won't fit. Nowadays, Japanese are about
the same height as your average American, but 150 or more years ago you're
looking at more like 4'11. Unfortunately the armour Tom wore was the worst
in the movie. The Dou was poorly shaped and fitted. The lacing was terrible.
What you PROBABLY want is a fair modern reproduction. If I remember
correctly a nuinobe dou with everything will run about $5000 from Kozando if
you are 5'6 to 5'11. Over 6' you're probably looking at custom. More
specifics will allow me to get you some more info.
