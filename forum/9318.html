SamuraiWiki - approved editors

by kitsuno

The SamuraiWiki (http://wiki.samurai-archives.com) is now locked from
editing by new members. It goes without saying that you don't need to
sign up to READ everything on the site.

If you wish to become an approved editor, please join the SamuraiWiki
Editor's Group: http://groups.yahoo.com/group/samuraiwiki/

Post your interest in becoming an editor, what your
interests/specialties are in regards to Japanese history, what "areas"
of the SamuraiWiki you'd like to work in, and if applicable, feel
freet to mention any "qualifications". If approved, you will gain the
ability to edit any unlocked articles, and to create new articles.

These members are already Approved Editors:

http://wiki.samurai-archives.com/index.php?title=SamuraiWiki:Approved_Editors

We look forward to your help!
