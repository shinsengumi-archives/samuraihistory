Re: Weapons of Ninjas

by zevlord

Daniele is correct they are called Sai and Raphael did uses two.
However they are not Ninja weapons, in actual fact they were
originally farming implements that was used to plant seeds. Now I
don't know when but they got extended for battle and instead of
carrying two they actually carried THREE one in each hand and one
tucked into their belt. When entering battle they would run towards
there enemies throwing one of the sai's at the enemy then drew the
third one ready for combat.
I have 2 pare of Sai's and believe them to be deadly in experienced
hands. Sai's are the ultimate disarming weapons in my opinion.

Re: [samuraihistory] Re: Weapons of Ninjas

by umaryu

Hi Kunai is a tool used by crafts men of various
labouring proffessions.

When a ninja was employing a disguise (hensojutsu) as
a craftsman that would carry a kunai then he was more
suited to have this at his side than a sword or tanto.
Less possibility of attracting attention to him self.

In one of the shinobi no mono movies there is a great
scene where a ninja uses the kunai to chip the mortar
away from around a window to gain entry.

I would say you have more chance a ninja would use it
as a tool/weapon than a smaurai. But at the end of the
day you would pick up anything lying around to use in
a situation if needed.

kunai are not knives.

they are metal slabs hammered out to make a type of
trowel. the edges are not even sharp.

the tipes may be pointed but at the end of the day if
your jamming the thing between rocks and into the

get a friction burn before it cut me.

that ryuu-ha only.

word brings up enough discussion from time to time that it's
probably impossible to declare with finality what is the 'right'
pronunciation.
