RE: [samuraihistory] Re: Autrocities in Seden by the Catholic Churc? Plz Explain... Was: Tokugawa Evil?

by mijalo_kr

Why did the Mongols attempt to invade Japan twice in the
thirteenth century? Why did they invade anywhere? Why does
anyone? Resources, imperialism, recognition as a dominant
race were all factors. Prior to the two assaults, the
Mongols had dispatched ambassadors to the Bakufu,
requesting/demanding the submission of the Japanese to a
position of inferiority, both of which were rather harshly
manhandled. When diplomatic bullying failed, the Mongols
decided fatefully to put their fleets into action.

Michael Lorimer
