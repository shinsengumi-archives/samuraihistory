Andrea,
You should check out the book "Legends of the Samurai" by Hiroaki Sato.
It's a great read, but most of the happenings in it are more realistic
than fantasy. Definately a good start. Also, head out to Barnes and
Noble (or Amazon) and check the mythology section for books like
"Japanese Fairy Tales" by Keiske Nishimoto and other such books. A
little study of Shinto will reveal some interesting Japanese tales as well.
_Joe
