Dear All,

here is a forwarded message from the Director of the Central Honshu and
kinki region of the All Japan Swordsmiths Association. I know some people
are interested.

P

Dear Sir/Madam

How are you? I hope you are enjoying your 'golden week' holiday. Very soon,
we
are holding an exhibition in Paris, France, by some of the young members of
the Japanese Art Sword Preservation Society. The exhibition is of blades by
the eight swordsmiths of the Murakumokai at the Japanese-French cultural
centre in Paris.

Please accept this invitation to come and see our exhibition. The exhibition
dates are the 21st and 22nd of May, 2005. The exhibition is only for two
days. If you want to come and see our work and deepen your
understanding of the Japanese sword and speak to us.

The swordsmiths, Kubo Yoshihiro, Matsuba Kunimasa, Yamamoto Tadashi and
myself
Mikami Tadanori will all be in attendance at the event and look forward to
meeting you and answering your questions.

This is the homepage of the Japanese-French culture centre for the "the
third
world Children's Day" event.
http://www.koinobori.org/Koinobori2005-jp.html

and the homepage of the Murakumokai (the heavenly gathering of clouds
society)
http://www.murakumokai.jp/

Yours sincerely

Mikami Koukei
