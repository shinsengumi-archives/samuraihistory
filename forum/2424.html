Hi Ezekiel,

The book you might want to check out is:
"IKKI," by James white, Cornell University 1995
Unfortunately, it's not so easy to find, and quite pricey.

Alternatively, there is a very good article ("Ikki in late medieval
Japan") in:
"MEDIEVAL JAPAN," edited by Hall and Mass, Standford, 1974
This is usually readily available, and should not cost more than $20.-.
The other essays in this book are quite good too.

Hope it helps,
Cepo, Honolulu.
