From what I understand Benkei's entire character is considered a legend.
I'd have to look back through the introduction to McCullough's
translation of the Yoshitsune legend but I'm fairly certain this is the
case. I remember reading once that Yoshitsune did have a monk companion
so this is probably where the legend grew from, as most of the people
(real or not) in Yoshitsune's party had their characters fleshed out by
later stories.
