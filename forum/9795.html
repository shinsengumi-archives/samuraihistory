I'm interested to that response... Of course, Akechi Mitsuhide was an interesting fellow for a number of reasons, but the reference to bushido and samurai fighting styles makes me wonder. Mitsuhide was one of the last people I'd be association with a code of honor.

Having said that... The Lamers biography on Oda Nobunaga (Japonius Tyrannus) is an excellent English-language resource that has a good deal of information about Akechi Mitsuhide, although much is related directly to his relationship with Nobunaga himself. I would also suspect that the accounts of the Portuguese missionaries like Frois (Historia de Japam) and Rodriguez (varoius sources) might have information in them too if you can get a translation. Otherwise, just check the Samurai Archives using the link below.

Jonathan P. Ellgen
