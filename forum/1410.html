Re: research project

by kitsuno

This book looks like it might be applicable:
http://www.yale.edu/yup/books/049676.htm

ARMED MARTIAL ARTS OF JAPAN
Swordsmanship and Archery
G. C. Hurst III

It goes into the history of the Japanese martial arts, how they
changed during the tokugawa period, and were 'sportified' (to use an
non-scholarly term) by westernization. and coming from Yale, I have
to assume it will be a worthy read, and not, as some have termed, a
book of "martial-arts mythology". If anyone has read it, I'd be
interested to hear about it.
