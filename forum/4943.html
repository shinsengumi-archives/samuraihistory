Re: [samuraihistory] samurai armour

by samurai_daimyo2003

Hello Mark

I'll try , First do as much research as you can ,
collect books that feature Samurai and also down load
pics from the internet, Study the many types of armour
and if you want to make a set for your self then
settle down with that model and with that your
research begins again, research material, assemply
techniques and so on. Local museums may have some that
you can get a close up and may even allow for you to
take a pic of it on display. Also consider just what
you intend to do with your armour. Is it display, or
is for living history like I'm trying to do now ? Will
it need to function like the real thing? or will it be
stood in a class case to be admired ? Also you need to
consider your sizes and how all the armour comes
together. Dont forget what needs to be worn under it
all. Thats starting from the ground up. Underware to
finally your sword. Each piece needs hours of research
and development. Good luck and have fun.

Takara
