Hello Andres,

my name is Zoltán Géczi. Unfortunatelly I don't have any significant
information on this question. If I could help you, I would - I used to paint
figures for long years, and I know those figure which you are busy with.
Great stuff, so do it nice. Please, let me tell you something, just an
advise: do not overdo the figure, keep it simple and elegant. Most painters
like to overcolor the samurai figures.
All the best,

GZ
