The shinsengumi are my favorite group in Japanese history. If you are searching for info about them... Read Hillsborough's Shinsengumi: The Shongun's Last Samurai Corps. I will list a few names, although I dont have all the information without refreshing my memory:

Kondo Izami
Serizawa Kamo
Shinmi Nishiki
Hijikata Toshizo
Yamamami Keisuke
Saito Hajime
Okita Soji
Nagakura Shinpachi
Harada Sanosuke
Todo Heisuke
Yamazaki Susumu
Hirayama Goro
Hirama Jusuke
Shimada Kai

There were originally 13 members, but I dont know which of these were in that group.

Jonathan Ellgen
