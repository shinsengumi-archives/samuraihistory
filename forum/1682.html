kokomans_2,

Despite the time period of the class not coinciding with "Feudal Japan" I
can offer one good book on the daily life of the Samurai, as well as
everyone else in Japan at that time. The book is called "Everyday Things in
Premodern Japan: The Hidden Legacy of Material Culture" and it is by Susan
B. Hanley ISBN: 0520218124

As for the "training," a very brief overview (academically speaking) can
be found in the book "Secrets of the Samurai: A survey of the Martial Arts
of Feudal Japan" by Ratti and Westbrook ISBN: 0785810730

I hope that this helped you in some way. If I can assist you further
please let me know.
