Re: [samuraihistory] Sengoku jidai..

by tatsushu

Sen = Cent - "T"
Go = Go
ku = Clue - "L"

Ji = Gee
Dai = Die

Sen-go-ku Ji-dai

Make sense? Japanese is pretty straight forward. Abominations such
as karaoke = Karryoki are English mutations.
