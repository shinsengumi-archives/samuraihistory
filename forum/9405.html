In the anime noted above the story is set four years after the (gomen
the spelling onegai) battle of 'Chekigahara' (Shek-ee-ga-har-a) and
there is a big Samurai kenjutsu (sword fighting) tournament being held
by the Shogun of the Toshowgowa Clan. My question is this--what year
was that battle and was there ever such a tournament? Who won? The
anime concerns a Battousai or Legendary manslayer nicknamed 'Demon Eyes
Kyo' whose spirit/soul inhabits the body of a younger hero named
Yushira. Looks like a cool series and I'm told it actually has an
ending after 24 eppys. I think that Shina the gal bounty hunter's 3
barrelled flintlock pistol is just smoke and mirrors and was never a
real weapon since flintlocks do not have rotating barrels. Domo arigato
for your assistance. Kami bless you all. Sayonara-K&K
