I have to relist one of the below books because of a "non-paying
bidder" (AKA scumbag, bastard, jerk, etc.), so here are the details:

Oda Nobunaga, by Kazuya Kudo
http://tinyurl.com/pl3lg

This is the first issue of the manga series "Oda Nobunaga" by Kazuya Kudo.

The first issue covers the events leading up to and immediately
following the battle of Okehazama, and is historical fiction. No
flying ninja or magic spells, it is a dramatization of the life of Oda
Nobunaga.

The manga is protected by a full color glossy dustcover, and the manga
itself is black and white with 208 pages. It is standard manga size,
approx. 6x8 inches, and is in good condition, barely any shelf wear.
The manga is in Japanese, imported from Japan.

Enjoy!
