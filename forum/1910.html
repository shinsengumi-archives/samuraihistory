Think about it...

by soshuju

Thinking about coming to SanFrancisco for the big sword show in August?

It just got a whole lot easier to get around; as of Sunday BART our
local rapid transit system goes all the way to Millbrae, just south
of the airport and a short 3 dollar cab ride from the Marriot.

Now you can stay anywhere in the bay area at the cheapest hotel you
like and you won't need to rent a car to see all those undiscovered
treasures! The Steehead Brewery is the same distance away from the
train station so at the end of the day you don't need to go home
hungry or most importantly THIRSTY!

Hope to see you all there...
