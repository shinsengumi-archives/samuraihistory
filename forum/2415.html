by darktwistedsamurai

The first episode of Rouroni Kenshin, when Kaouro is being harrased by
the group of men who begin mocking her desire to teach the use of the
sword as a tool of protection.
Kenshin walks into the dojo and says something to the effect of, "It
is true that the sword and the art of swordsmanship are tools of
death, and what Miss Kaouro says are the sweet and innocent words of
one who has never slain a man. However, I would like it very much to
be the truth one day, that I would."
