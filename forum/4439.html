Hello

My name is David Erickson , Im new to this yahoo group. About a week
since joining. Im very impressed with the group so far , Thanks for
having me aboard. I have a living history society called the
M.R.S.N.E.(Military Re-neactment Society of New England) one of our
affiliated living history groups is called Daimyo of the Samurai . It
is dedicated to the History, religion, and the way of the Samurai and
will express its self during time line events in costume as Samurai
and as the Samurai families. In the spring 2005 I plan to purchase a
horse and complete a mounted Samurai impression to show off during
time line event. Also along with the armor and weapons. I also have
studied martial arts for the past 20 yrs in Kendo, kung fu and Tae
Kwon Do. I am in the research phase now; to learn every thing I can
about every aspect of the Samurai, This Yahoo group has already been
a great help to me in so far as research. I will have many questions
in the near future.
