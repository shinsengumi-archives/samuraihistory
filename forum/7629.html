To anyone who might be interested in the life of Miyamoto Musashi. Just type
in "Miyamoto Musashi" into Google or any other search engine. You will come up
with some sites that offer historical information about his life. They are
varied and you simply have to use your logic to figure out which ones make the
most sense after reading several of them.

You can even read Miyamoto's own version of his life in his "Book Of Five
Rings." Which is widely available, translated into several languages.

Speaking of individuals, this is one individual whose life fascinates me.
