Samurai History Game

by kevinburns_jp

What is your favourite game about Samurai history?

I live in Kanagawa, Japan and have done for many years. I like it
here.

So far my favourite is Shogun or Samurai Swords by Milton Bradley.
But I haven`t played so many yet. I have also played a Japanese
game called Sekigahara by 6 Angles. Another game by 6 Angles
depicted the Mongol invasion of Japan--the one where the "Kamikaze"
or Divine Wind intervened. Another good simulation--it game one the
feeling of being there, as much as a game can.

Your thoughts on games please.

Re: [samuraihistory] Samurai History Game

by ltdomer98

Shogun is a decent game--the second time they released
it they called it "Samurai Swords", for whatever
reason. It's the best board game about Japan I've
seen...not familiar with too many others.

I'm a big fan of Shogun:Total War and it's follow-ups,
though they aren't the greatest accuracy wise, they
make up for it in game play. And of course if you have
access to Japanese games, the Nobunaga's Ambition
(Nobunaga no Yabou) series is still going strong (just
bought a new one a week or so ago!)

Nate

Samurai History Game

by scott_rekishika

There is a way to get Nobunagas Ambition on your computer. go to
www.romnantion.net. Then click emulaators, and then pick the genesis
one. Sfter you download the emulator, click on roms on the right
hand side and pick Genesis. THe games are arranged in alphabetical
order. They have a lot of games for genesis, and they do not take up
much space on the hard drive. To play you open your genesis
emulators, and then click file, open rom, and then pick the game that
you have saved to your computer. Kind of a round about way to get it
I guess, but it is free.
