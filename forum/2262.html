I'm pretty sure all you have to do is increase your bid until you get
to the reserve price, until that time, it won't matter, but once you
raise it up to the reserve price, you'll be the high bidder. At
least that is how I understand it. The EBAY FAQ should tell you
exactly how it works. I've never created a reserve auction, but I've
bid in them before.
