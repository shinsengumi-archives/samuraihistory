If it is indeed Tangrams you are talking about....
tangrams are usually four or five triangles of different sizes, a square,
and a parallelogram that fit together to make a square. They can easily be
made at home. A quick google search (
http://www.google.com/search?hl=en&ie=ISO-8859-1&q=tangrams ) yields:
http://www.tangrams.ca/
http://www.strongmuseum.org/kids/tangram.html
and
http://mathforum.org/trscavo/tangrams/construct.html
