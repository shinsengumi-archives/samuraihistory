Don't take this wrong, but... um....

You're writing a book on Yoritomo and yet you're asking people whose
veracity and limit of knowledge is untestable to tell you about him? Aren't
you doing research?

And, for the record, it is arguable that Yoritomo had little or nothing to
do with the shogunate as it eventually evolved. The Muromachi shogunate was
the product of Hojo engineering, and was a very different structural and
functional animal from the shogunate of the Tokugawa, for example.
