ago when I asked about the movie on DVD. I can tell that guy didn't
like it, and I think he sees Kurasawa as god, at least he acts like

me to see this as possible. 2 days for meengers to arrive, time to
mobilize the garrison, and then 2 days back, and the almost
invetiable delays in the actual travel. Just something that has been
on my mind and wanted to speak my peace.
