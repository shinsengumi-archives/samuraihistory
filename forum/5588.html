Japnese Spears

by Clive Sinclaire

Gentlemen and Ladies
I attendfed a lecture and book launch today given by Roald and Patricia Knutsen for their jointly written new book JAPANESE SPEARS - Polearms and their use in old Japan. It is, quote "a historical survey of Naginata and Yari from the earliest times to the close of the Edo period".
Published by Global Oriental Ltd I am not sure where it will be available but the publisher's web-site is www.globaloriental.co.uk.
I have not, of course read it yet but we will be reviewing it for To-ken Society of Great Britain in the near future.
Regards
Clive Sinclaire
