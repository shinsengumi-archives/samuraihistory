Hi Andrea

There are several excellent books and sources regarding legends. For a start try "The Nobility of Failure" - Ivan Morris: this work chronicles several personalities from the 4th - 20th centuries. Maybe even "Musashi" - Eiji Yoshikawa: An epic novel of the samurai era {so its blurb states..} Or even look at some of the Japanese Woodcuts, such as those of Kuniyoshi to find legend topics.
Good luck with the writing....
