Re: [samuraihistory] Re:Himeji-jo Occupants

by William Letham

After Ikeda,
Honda Tadamasa 1617-39
Matsudaira Tadaaki and his son Tadahiro 1639-49
Matsudaira Naomoto and his son Naohiro 1648-49
Sakakibara Tadatsugu and his son Masafusa 1649-82
Honda Tadakuni and his son Tadataka 1682-1704
Sakakibara Masakuni 1704-1749
Sakai Tadasumi 1749-succeeded by eight descendants until the Bakumatsu
period.

These are the names I could find I think that there are more names but I
believe these are the families that ruled Himeji-Jo during the Edo period.
By the way it was Tokugawa Ieyasu that bestowed the castle onto Ikeda,
Toyotomi Hideyoshi was the hegemon that ruled before Tokugawa
