I don't know about how biographical it is, but where I started getting info
on him was through the Capcom PlayStation 2 game Oni-Musha 2: Samurai's
Destiny (which, admittedly, takes A LOT of liberties with life dates, as the
game was set in 1570 or 1573, and Jubei wasn't born until the 1600's). From
what little I've read on the actual man, he was born in 1606 to a sword
instructor in the Tokugawa government, and himself grew to be a great
swordsman. From that same source (an on-line edition of a Japanese magazine,
which one I am not sure), he died in 1650, possibly in either a battle or a
duel.

find out more about Saiga (or Saigo) Magoichi?

Hey,
Saiga, or Saigo, Magoichi was a real person from the Sengoku era. If
memory serves me correctly, and I do not remember where I read it although it
was online, he was a captain, or leader in some way, of a gun squadron in the
Kii area, and may have been somehow associated with a group of monks there.
Additionally, although it doesn't really matter or apply: He made an appearance
in Capcom's best game ever, Onimusha 2: Samurai's Destiny in a supporting
role, where he is referred to as "captain of the Kiishi Saiga Gun Team". Hope
this helps a little. :-) Take care. :-)
