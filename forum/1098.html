Re: [samuraihistory] History Question

by Mikiko Letham

There were quite a few small scale rebellions during the Edo period mostly
during poor harvest periods. I don't recall what weapons they used.
Shimabara's revolt involved ronin as well as peasants. Some books that might
help 'Peasant protests and uprisings in Tokugawa Japan' Stephan Vlastos,
'Peasant Protest in Japan' Herbert Bix. Both books focus on the social
conditions of the revolts, not on the military aspects.
