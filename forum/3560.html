Re: [samuraihistory] Re: Choshu han ryu?

by soshuju

Kyle et al-
The upper class members of the Choshu han studied Yagyu Shinkage
Ryu,(Takasugi Shinsaku was a Yagyu Shinkage Ryu swordman...) I haven't
been able to find a ryuha for the Middle and lower classes prior to
Kaei. At which time Saito Shintaro established a Shindo Munen Ryu dojo
there with the permission of the Mori. The aforementioned Katsura
Kogoro was a Shindo Munen ryu adept who became well known in Edo
circles thru his fencing ability...
