Re: [samuraihistory] Videos?

by S. Lyle Parker

I did see a documentary on the history channel a year or two ago. It was
not particularly scholarly, but of some use as an introductory look at the
development of the Japanese warrior over time. The documentary included
excerpts pf interviews with a few of the "biggies": Stephen Turnbull, G.
Cameron Hurst and Karl Friday. I did a search on the history channel
website and came up with the following:

In Search of History:
Samurai Warrior
Price: $19.95
http://www.historychannel.com

This seems a bit steep of a price from what I remember of the content, but
perhaps someone else on the list could give a more detailed accounting. One
caveat, I seem to recall an email conversation with Prof. Friday in which he
intimated that some of his statements were taken a bit out of context.

Cheers,

Shannon
