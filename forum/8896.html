I've watched quite a few of them. Most are based on novelized
historical fiction, so they are a mix of history, known or accepted
myth, with added modern twists to interest the next generation and
women in them. Such things as Mori Motonari's dedication to Mii-no-
kata and swearing off concubines is pure fiction (from Mori Motonari),
but tossed in to make it more date-friendly I suppose.
