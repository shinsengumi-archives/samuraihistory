Off Topic Message

by jin_gai_guy

Dear List

Please forgive me for this vaguely sword related post, and lend me your
signatures. Also, please forgive my typos made in haste while trying save a
truly historic dojo, they are being addressed.

It is time to fight the man again. Multi million dollar Kodansha publishing
company want to tear down Noma dojo (Tokyo) as it does not suit them to have
a historically important dojo on their grounds. Before the war, Noma dojo
was one of the four great dojo of the eastern capital. Now it is the only
remaining one. It was the site of the last kendo competition that was
attended by the Emperor, and the dojo of the most famous sensei of the 20th
century Mochida Moriji sensei.

Please support our opposition to tearing down such a wonderful dojo and
memorable experience for visiting kendoka. Here is a link for our petition
to save Noma dojo.

http://www.petitiononline.com/savenoma/petition.html

Best

Paul
