Re: [samuraihistory] Aoyama family crest

by rodjohnson001

Hi Coeli,

There are many family crests (Mon) associated with the Aoyama. Of these I can readily identify five mon, the easiest to describe without a pic would be a circle with eight circles touching and surrounding it - a little like the Hosakawa. A further example would be a single coin (zeni); this is a circle with a small square cut out in the centre. To be a little more accurate you would need to identify the branch of the family or province in which they lived.

I could get some scan of these off to you if you need them.

Regards,

Rod Johnson
