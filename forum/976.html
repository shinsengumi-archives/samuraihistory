Re: [samuraihistory] Other good Samrai groops

by Tom Helm

My interest is primarily Japanese swords and related arts. Some armour
study, all of which means one must study history. If you are interested in
swords I can recommend both groups below. The first if like the name says
for Yahoos! lots of good natured joking and fun, several knowledgeable
people and often some otherwise hard to find info.
The second is focused on art swords and while not snobbish is a bit more
serious about it. The list master is an avowed expert on modern blades so
this is often a subject there. I invite anyone who is interested to drop in
or is that on?

Tom
