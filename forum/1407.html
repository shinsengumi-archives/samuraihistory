Battle of Kawanakajima Re-enactment

by kitsuno

(I saw the following on Japanese TV a few years ago, pretty
interesting)


"The Battle of Kawanakajima 2003 " will be held in Isawa-cho,
Yamanashi prefecture, Japan.

On 20 April 2003, Approximately 800 volunteers and semi professionals
will perform the Battle of Kawanakajima,
which took place in the 16 century in the middle part of Japan.
You can participate either as a performer or a spectator.
If you are lucky and strong enough, you can be a Taisho(a captain),
if not a foot soldier.

Japan Airlines recommend a special interest tour for this event,
Please see the below web address and find the title "The Battle of
Kawanakajima".
You can get more information and tour details.
http://www.jal.co.jp/japantravelinfo/

If you are interested in joining this tour,
please contact us at anytime.
