On Ebay - the Takeda Clan's Koyo Gunkan

by kitsuno

I have two copies of the Koyo Gunkan, so I've listed one for sale on Ebay. It is the cleanest and newest of my two copies, in very excellent condition. Buying it on amazon.co.jp would probably cost well over $50, so hopefully this is a good deal:

http://cgi.ebay.com/ws/eBayISAPI.dll?ViewItem&item=190324387743

This is a a near-pristine copy of the Takeda clan's "Koyo Gunkan" a comprehensive history of the Takeda clan of 16th century Japan and manual of war and philosophy. Each chapter includes first the original archaic Japanese text followed by a modern Japanese translation, and a comprehensive introduction gives details on the history of the Takeda clan, clan genealogy, and a history of the circumstances of the Koyo Gunkan. The book is hardcover in excellent condition, and comes with a hardcover case which is in great condition with a little shelf wear.

Chapters include:

The Strategist Yamamoto Kansuke
The Removal of Takeda Nobutora from Kai Province
The Destruction of Murakami Yoshikiyo at Uedahara
Battles at Kawanakajima
Tokugawa Ieyasu and Oda Nobunaga at Mikatagahara
Shingen's last campaign
A Hero Falls
Destruction at Nagashino
The End of the Takeda Clan
