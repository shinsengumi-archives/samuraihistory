RE: [samuraihistory] Bakufu

by Haynes, A (Angus)

I'd recommend William Wayne Farris' book "Heavenly Warriors" for
information on the formation of the Kamakura Bakufu. For the Ashikaga
Bakufu I'd recommend Sansom's "A History of Japan: 1334-1615".

I haven't read a lot on the Tokugawa Bakufu, but Sansom's "A History of
Japan: 1615-1867" probably covers it nicely, as well.

All of these books should be relatively easy to track down, and all are
listed on Amazon.com

HTH,

Angus
