Sono Toki.... Oda Nobunaga and Saito Dosan

by kitsuno

Just finished watching this weeks episode of "Sono toki rekishi ga
ugoita". It examined the relationship between Oda Nobunaga and Saito
Dosan, as well as the 4 years leading up to Nobunaga's invasion of
Mino. It said recent scholarship points to Nobunaga being heavily
influenced by Dosan (as opposed to his father, Oda Nobutora), who was
very brutal in his own right. Definately an interesting show, anyone
with NHK should take a look, although next week is Lawrence of
Arabia...
