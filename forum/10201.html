Are you talking about Sakura Sogoro?  Took place in what is now Chiba Prefecture (Boso Hanto back then).  Taxes too high, he put a sign up on a bridge the daimyo was to pass iirc, taxes were lowered and he and his family were put to death from what I recall.
 
http://forums.samurai-archives.com/viewtopic.php?t=434
 
http://www005.upp.so-net.ne.jp/aoh/SAKURA.HTM
4th paragraph there
 
Kabuki play on it:  http://kabuki21.com/sakura_giminden.php
 
Nice little fantastical illustration here:  http://dappan.net/research/tanaka/2.html
 
There's a place in Chiba prefecture called Sakura (visited it when I was a boy and didn't pay as much attention as I should have) - believe it's a city now.  Has a shrine called the Sogo Reid Sanctuary (Toshoji, which the site calls Toshoji temple which is like saying Tosho temple temple):  http://www.chiba-tour.jp/narita/sight/sight_top.html
 
Hope this helps.
