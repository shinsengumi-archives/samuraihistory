I didn't know but looking around the web, it appears her name was 真喜
姫 which I guess would be read Maki-hime.

This is from http://nekhet.ddo.jp/people/japan/tokugawa01.html

Another site says clearly that they had no children together:
http://homepage2.nifty.com/hpsuiren/sengoku/tokugawa/yasu-1.htm

It seems that Hirotada married Maki in 1545 and died in early 1549.
Considering his campaigning during the interim perhaps they had no
time to have kids.

Yasumitsu was 24 when he died in 1549. Maki lived on until 1571.

Hope this helps a little.
