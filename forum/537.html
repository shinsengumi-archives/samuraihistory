Saludos a Portugal!


The most accessible books on the introduction to Samurai culture are
as follows:

"SAMURAI WARFARE" by Turnbull
"THE SAMURAI SOURCEBOOK" by Turnbull
"SAMURAI 1550-1600" by Bryant (Osprey Warrior series)
"SEKIGAHARA 1600" by Bryant (Osprey Campaign series)
"NAGASHINO 1575" by Turnbull (Osprey Campaign series)

Unfortunately, I have yet to find a book which deals with correct
orders of battle, precise use of Sashimono by units and so on. This
is very frustrating for the wargamer, much more than painting the
lacing on armour! Hey Nuno, have you seen the Perry's new figures?

Regards,


Joe

PS I have not heard of the use of Christian Samurai by the
Portuguese, Mendes Pinto's initial influence on Samurai warfare is
the only Samurai-Portuguese military connection I know of, which of
course does not mean there were not others!
