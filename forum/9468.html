by Barry Thomas

James,

In your reply I notice a space between "geocities" and ".com" in the url address. This should not be there. Press and hold your left mouse button and run the cursor over that entire address line and copy it to the clipboard (press "Ctrl" and "C" together). Then paste it into your browser address bar (click to put the cursor there - the entire address bar should then be selected - then press "Ctrl" and "V" together to paste the address AND REMOVE ANY SPACES (I see another space between the 'i' and 'n' in "wind"). Then press "Enter" and it should work. It appears to have worked for others already. Please get back to me offline if this does not work.

Regards,

Barry Thomas.
