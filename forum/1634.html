Re: [samuraihistory] Japanese history book source

by William letham

There is a National Diet Library. They just opened a branch in Nara and I'm
planning to go soon. I'm not sure where or how you can contact them. But if
you do a search for 'National Diet Library Japan' you are sure to get some
hits. Whether you can get material from them is another matter. I did an
interlibrary search for a book at my branch library and they found it at the
National Diet Library. They sent it to my branch but I couldn't take it out
or make copies. So I spent a whole afternoon reading with screaming kids
running around.

by burker94509

Eleven years ago, I wrote to the National Diet Library and purchased
photocopies of the Japan Weekly. It was a weekly English-language newspaper
published in Japan. I purchased the issues that mentioned the Satsuma
Rebellion.

Here is the address I have for the National Diet Library:

National Diet Library
1-10-1 Nagata-cho Chiyoda-ku
Tokyo 100
Japan

Bob Burke
