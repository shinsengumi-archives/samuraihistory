Here is the third ebay fundraiser auction:

Battle Royale / Azumi - 3 issues.

These are three imported Japanese Manga that are based on the Japanese
movies "Battle Royale", and "Azumi". There are two issues of "Azumi" -
Issue #5 and #7.

Battle Royale is the first issue of the series that follows a
highschool graduating class forced to fight to the death, and is in
excellent condition, with a full color dust jacket in great condition
and is 209 pages. This issue was published in 2001.

The issues of Azumi are #5 and #7, and are in good condition, with
some shelf wear on the dust jackets. Both issues have full color dust
jackets, and were published in 2003, and have approximately 206 pages
each. Azumi follows the story of a female assassin during the Sengoku
period of Japanese history.

All three are Japanese imports and are the original Japanese language
editions.
