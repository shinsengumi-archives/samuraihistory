Samurai Wiki's Most Wanted

by kitsuno

Currently we need volunteers to fill out some categories on the
SamuraiWiki, so if anyone is interested come and sign up:
http://wiki.samurai-archives.com/

PLEASE READ THE HELP SECTIONS IF YOU ARE NOT FAMILIAR WITH EDITING
WIKI ARTICLES:
http://wiki.samurai-archives.com/index.php?title=SamuraiWiki:Help
People who contribute without reading the help sections consistently
and always make a mess that the Admins have to take time formatting
and cleaning up. Please save us the trouble and browse similar
articles to the one you want to create so that we don't have to clean
up and reformat your article because you didn't follow the standards
and formats.

When you sign up, if you do not contribute anything for a day or two,
you will find a verification box on your user page. If you do not
remove it within 15 days, your account will be blocked as inactive and
a possible spam account. If it is blocked in error, email me, and I
will unblock it as soon as possible. This is only done to prevent
spam accounts. Once you remove the box from your user page, or if you
contribute, you will be verified permanently. If you don't think you
will contribute for a long time, or at all, don't create an account
until you are ready to contribute. You do not need to create an
account in order to read and browse articles.

Also, plagarism is absolutely not tolerated, and sources must always
be cited. Failure to do so may result in banning.

Here are some categories that need volunteers to create articles,
however any articles are always appreciated:

Fiction Books
http://wiki.samurai-archives.com/index.php?title=Category:Fiction_Books
Including Citation info, synopsis, and purchase links.

Bujutsu articles
http://wiki.samurai-archives.com/index.php?title=Category:Bujutsu
A lot of the articles are small stubs and also need sources. If you
want to help out with the Bujutsu articles, feel free to join the
"Bujutsu Task Force":
http://wiki.samurai-archives.com/index.php?title=SamuraiWiki:Bujutsu_task_force

Movies:
http://wiki.samurai-archives.com/index.php?title=Category:Movies
There are tons of Samurai and historical movies out there that need to
be added. Including info such as cast list, synopsis, crew list, etc.

Other sections that also need to be filled out are the Mythology
sections, including dieties, folklore, and obake:
http://wiki.samurai-archives.com/index.php?title=Category:Mythology

The largest categories so far (of course) mostly deal with the sengoku
period, including:

Sengoku Period:
http://wiki.samurai-archives.com/index.php?title=Category:Sengoku_Period

Samurai:
http://wiki.samurai-archives.com/index.php?title=Category:Samurai

Clans:
http://wiki.samurai-archives.com/index.php?title=Category:Clans

Timeline:
http://wiki.samurai-archives.com/index.php?title=Category:Timeline

Battles:
http://wiki.samurai-archives.com/index.php?title=Category:Battles

REMEMBER: You do not need to sign up as a user to READ articles, ONLY
to contribute. Enjoy!!
