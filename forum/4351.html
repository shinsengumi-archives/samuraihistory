>Hi Natan,

Welcome to this group.

Have you read TANEGASHIMA: The Arrival of Europe in Japan by Olof G. Lidin? published by Nias Press (www.niaspress.dk)

I recently bought a copy from Kinokuniya (the Japanese chain bookstore) but have only had time to skim it. It seems to have a very good bibliography and notes.

Anyone else here read it? Opinions?

Also some time ago I read Deus Destroyed by George Ellison which gives a very dramatic picture of the differences between European and Japanese religious belief and ways of thinking.

A very useful Japanese history bibliography page is

http://www.oriental.cam.ac.uk/jbib/bibtitle.html

Hope this is of some help - sorry can't write more for you!!
