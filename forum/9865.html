The ninja assassin story is indeed very popular. The ninja was said
to have hid in the lavatory, more like a latrine for three days
waiting for his target to come to that particular one, when the
target did indeed arrive he was stabbed from below [yes the ninja was
stood in the actual sewer stream] and killed.

This story blossomed from the rumours that the Oda had him
assassinated. This was mainly due to the timely death which benefited
the Oda enormously.

However, many Japanese scholars believe he contracted a severe
illness, stomach cancer is mentioned, and thereby died.

The legend of the assassination is a little more colourful but a lot
less likely - but who knows
