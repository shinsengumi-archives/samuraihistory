Answer to Question.2.
The War of 1180-85 ended with the annihilation of the
Taira and their infant Emperor, the infant Antoku . As
a result Minamoto no Yoritomo became the predominant
warlord, removing the potential threat of his brother
Yoshitsune and establishing himself, ably handling
relations between himself, the religious sects, and
the imperial Court . As a result, he established the
Kamakura bakufu (shogunate) in 1192 .
Hope this helps
Michael Lorimer
