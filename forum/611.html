by S. Lyle Parker

Dear All,

There is an online "repository" of academic journals called JSTOR (forget
what the acronym means), and if your local state college library has a
subscription then you can search through the asian (or other) studies
journals for articles and print them out for free. Of course, this would
have to be done from one of the libraries computers...
