Hmmmm. We just had a similar discussion over at the Samurai History Forum;
so I might have given this info to you before.

In English the only detailed work on Nagamasa and Japanese involvement in
Thailand/Siam I have found is;
"Notes on the Intercourse between Japan and Siam in the Seventeenth century"
by Ernest M Satow, Transactions of the Asiatic Society of Japan, Vol. 13, pt
II, pages 139-210

In Japanese there are oodles but the only thing I have is volume 140 of the
Visual Nihon Rekishi Series by DeAgustini on Nagamasa.

I hope this helps.
