It all depends on what sort of relationship you want to portray. If
you want to portray your figures as belonging to the same group, then
paint the sashimono banners (the banners on poles behind their back)
and the little banners on the back of their helmets more or less the
same. The sashimono banners should probably have kamon on them. The
little helmet banners probably should not.
