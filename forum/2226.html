This showed up on another list; Any Welsh Samurai out there?

You might be interested in this if you're anywhere near Cardiff, and
apologies if you're not (though you may be able to get to see the film
some other way if you contact the director):

Samurai Monogatari, a 22-minute film made by a Welsh film-maker, will be
shown at Chapter Arts Centre in Cardiff on Saturday 13th 1.30pm. It's the
first Samurai film ever made in Wales.

The film is in Japanese with English subtitles, and features six Japanese
actors who work and study in Cardiff.

Tickets are free but by invitation only. If you'd like a ticket, contact
the director Gareth Evans at <jpreview_contact@...>.
