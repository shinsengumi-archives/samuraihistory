samurai booklets

by robbroomuk

After a couple of off line requests, heres a bit more detail on the
Killer Katana series of clan booklets for every body.

The KK booklets have around 24 pages.
Several pages gives clan details and history.

You then have orders of battle for two battles, plus where possible
information on Samurai in service at the time (a list of names).
Then information on the battles including some maps, and some Mon and
heraldry details.
I think they cost around 6 dollars US or 6 pounds UK sterling.

The bibliography also lists Rekishi Gunzo as a source for some of the
booklets.

Hope that helps

Kind regards
