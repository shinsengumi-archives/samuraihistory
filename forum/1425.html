Yet more genealogy questions

by kitsuno

Looking at the connection between the Tokugawa and the Maeda clans, I
find that a daughter of Tokugawa Hidetada (Ne- or Nene- hime [1599-
1622]) married Maeda Toshitsune [1593-1658]. does anyone know if his
sons Mitsumasa, Toshitsugi, and Toshiharu were sons of Hidetada's
daughter?

Second question - Hidetada's daughter Senhime married Honda
Tadatoshi, a nephew of Honda Tadamasa. Does anyone know who his
father was (which brother of Honda Tadamasa)?


Thanks for any assistance!

Re: Yet more genealogy questions

by kitsuno

Oops, disregard that. There were two Honda Tadatokis, the one who
died in 1615 was AKA Tadatomo, and was Honda Tadatoki's Uncle.
Solves that problem. The Tadatoki married to Senhime died in 1626.
Senhime was actually his half first cousin, once removed, by the
way.It looks like relations married with some frequency during the
sengoku period.
