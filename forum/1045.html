Personally, my favourite samurai is Minamoto-no
Yoshitsune . Yoritomo his brother was clearly more
pragmatic and managed to consolidate the Kamakura
bakufu and appease the rival Buddhist sects, but
Yoshitsune appears to me to be a more rounded samurai;
a warrior of undoubted loyalty and took offence to all
who did doubt it, in the end dying for his belief in
himself .
Michael Lorimer
