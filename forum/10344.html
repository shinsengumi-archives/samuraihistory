All -
Last minute reminder, the 2011 Northern California Japanese Sword
Club To-Ken Taikai is just a week away. Less than that really. The
largest sword show and sale outside of Japan, featuring dealers from
Japan, Europe, Australia and all over the United States and Canada. In
addition to over 100 dealers tables there will be educational events
presented by the Nihon Bijutsu To-ken Hozon Kyokai U.S. Shibu, the
Nihon To-ken Hozon Kai, and the Japanese Sword Society of the United
States. Attendance was up last year, the hotel is filling up and we
expect this year to be another busy and exciting event.
If you all are anywhere near San Francisco at this time of year you
should not miss it! I look forward to seeing you all there,
