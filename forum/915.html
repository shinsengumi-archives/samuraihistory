Some Ashigaru were supplied with Sashimono by their Daimyo. Some
Daimyo did not. Uniformed ashigaru seemed to be used during the main
period of Sengoku about 1570-1600. My dates might be a bit off but it
seems to me to coincide with the rise of professional ashigaru during
the later years. Each ashigaru would have one if so organized.
